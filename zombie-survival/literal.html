Zombie Survival Guide

FIGURATIVE LITERAL

Dress For Success

  • Comfortable clothes.
  • No high heels.
  • Motorcycle helmet with visor.
  • Leather jacket and gloves - zombies can’t chew through leather.

Best Defense

  • Machete - hacks off limbs and heads, never needs a reload.
  • Hatchet - a nice backup, doubles as a hammer.
  • Guns - as much guns and ammo as you can carry.

Other Supplies

  • Enough gas and food to get you from one city to another.
  • Rope - for tying doors closed when you are running through abandoned buildings.
  • Cleaning supplies - hand sanitizer & soap are going to come in handy after sweating in your leather all day fighting zombies.
  • Dental hygiene - the world may be going to hell in a hand basket, but you don't need a toothache, too.
  • Water and water containers - speaking of sweating all day, you’ll need plenty of water. Fill up anywhere you might wash up.
